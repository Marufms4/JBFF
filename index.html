Japan Bangladesh Friendship Foundation (JBFF)

The Name Implies A Collaborative Endeavor Between Two Countries Namely Bangladesh And Japan For Cooperating The People Of Bangladesh In Making A Skill Based Society Free From Repression,Unemployment,Want And Illiteracy. The Foundation Was Formed To Look After The Welfare Of The Community We Live In And Provide Basic Necessities Like Education, Medicare And Shelter To Those In Need, Empower Them To Build For Themselves A Better Life In Every Aspect. We Dream Of A Prosperous Bangladesh And A Society That Is Not Poor, Bangladesh Which Is Literate And Empowered. We Dream Of A Country Without Any Forms Of Vulnerability, Exploitation And Inefficiencies. We Dream Of Choices, Better Opportunities And Collaboration.
